Campus Services

Our university offers a variety of services to help students thrive academically, socially, and personally. Explore the wide range of services available to make the most of your university experience.

Library Services

The University Library is a central hub for students, offering access to thousands of books, e-books, research journals, and study spaces. We provide both physical and online resources to support your academic success.

  • Access to over 50,000 academic journals and publications.
  • 24/7 online access to digital resources.
  • Study rooms available for group and individual study sessions.
  • Library workshops on research, citations, and academic writing.

Student Counseling Services

Our counseling services offer students the support they need to manage academic stress, personal challenges, and mental well-being. All services are confidential and provided by licensed professionals.

  • One-on-one counseling sessions.
  • Group therapy sessions focused on personal development.
  • Workshops on stress management and time management.
  • Confidential consultations on academic pressure and career guidance.

Health Center

Our campus health center is here to provide students with healthcare services ranging from emergency medical care to routine check-ups. We also offer health education and wellness programs throughout the year.

  • On-campus medical assistance for minor illnesses and injuries.
  • Routine health check-ups and vaccinations.
  • Mental health support and counseling referrals.
  • Health and wellness workshops on topics like nutrition and fitness.

Student Dining Services

Our dining facilities offer nutritious, balanced meals for students at affordable prices. We have a variety of food options catering to diverse dietary needs and preferences.

  • Multiple dining halls with vegetarian, vegan, and halal options.
  • Healthy snack bars and grab-and-go meal options.
  • Cafeterias offering international and local cuisines.
  • Meal plans available for on-campus residents.

Campus Transportation

Our campus shuttle service ensures students can easily travel between different areas of the campus. The service is free for all students and runs on a regular schedule.

  • Shuttle buses operating throughout the day.
  • Real-time tracking for shuttle buses via mobile app.
  • Bike-sharing programs available for eco-friendly transportation.
  • Parking services for students with cars and motorcycles.

IT Support Services

Our IT support services are here to help students with any technical issues related to university systems, Wi-Fi connectivity, and personal devices. We offer both in-person and remote assistance.

  • 24/7 technical support for students.
  • Access to computer labs with high-speed internet.
  • Free software tools available for students (Microsoft Office, antivirus software).
  • Workshops on tech literacy and cybersecurity awareness.

Career Services

Our Career Services team offers guidance to help students transition from university life to their professional careers. We provide career counseling, job search support, and networking opportunities with employers.

  • Career counseling and resume-building workshops.
  • Internship and job placement opportunities.
  • Mock interviews and job fairs.
  • Alumni network access for career guidance and mentoring.
