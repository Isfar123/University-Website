Media Program

Our Media program provides a comprehensive education in journalism, broadcasting, digital media, and communication.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer the following undergraduate media programs:

  • Bachelor of Journalism
  • Bachelor of Media and Communication
  • Bachelor of Film and Television Production

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs offer advanced skills in media management, digital storytelling, and broadcasting:

  • Master of Media Studies
  • PhD in Media Studies (Research-based program)

Media Courses

The following are some of the core courses offered as part of the Media program:

Journalism Ethics

Study of ethical issues in journalism and media practices.

Digital Media Production

Techniques and tools for creating digital content for online platforms.

Media Law

Focus on the legal regulations governing the media industry.

Public Relations

Methods of managing and communicating an organization’s image.
