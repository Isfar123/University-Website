Business Administration Program

The Business Administration program equips students with essential skills for leadership roles in the corporate world.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer the following undergraduate business programs:

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Science in Management
  • Bachelor of Marketing

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate business programs prepare students for senior management roles and advanced business research:

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Science in International Business
  • PhD in Business Administration (Research-based program)
