Education Program

Our Education program provides future educators with the knowledge and skills to excel in teaching, leadership, and research.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer the following undergraduate education programs:

  • Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.)
  • Bachelor of Arts in Education

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs focus on advanced educational theories and practices:

  • Master of Education (M.Ed.)
  • PhD in Education (Research-based program)

Education Courses

The following are some of the core courses offered as part of the Education program:

Educational Psychology

Explores how psychological principles influence teaching and learning.

Curriculum Design

Methods of designing effective and inclusive educational curricula.

Pedagogical Practices

Focus on teaching strategies and techniques in the classroom.

Educational Leadership

Leadership and management in educational settings.
