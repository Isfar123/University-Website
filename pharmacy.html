Pharmacy Program

The Pharmacy program prepares students for careers in healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and research.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer the following undergraduate pharmacy programs:

  • Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm)
  • Bachelor of Pharmaceutical Sciences

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs provide advanced knowledge and research skills in pharmacy:

  • Master of Pharmacy (MPharm)
  • PhD in Pharmaceutical Sciences (Research-based program)
