Campus Services

At Independent University, Bangladesh, we strive to provide a wide range of services to support the academic and personal growth of our students. Our campus services are designed to help you excel in your studies, stay healthy, and have an enriching student life.

  • Library - Our well-equipped library offers a vast collection of books, journals, and online resources to assist you with your academic work.
  • Student Counseling - Professional counseling services are available for students to provide guidance on mental health and personal development.
  • Health Center - Our campus health center offers basic healthcare services and first aid to students.
  • Dining Services - A variety of healthy and affordable dining options are available in our campus cafeterias.
  • Transportation - Convenient campus shuttle services are available to help students commute around the university campus.
