Admission Period (Intakes)

Our university has multiple intake periods throughout the year to accommodate a wide range of applicants. The main intakes are:

  • Spring Intake: January to April
  • Summer Intake: May to August
  • Fall Intake: September to December

Applications are accepted for all intakes, and deadlines vary by program. Please check the specific program page for detailed deadlines.

Special Considerations: Exemption from Admission Test

In some cases, applicants may be exempt from the admission test based on their previous academic qualifications or professional experience. The following categories of students may apply for an exemption:

  • Applicants with a recognized undergraduate degree in a related field
  • Applicants with exceptional work experience or professional achievements in the relevant field
  • International students who meet specific criteria set by the university

Admission Requirements

General Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • High school diploma or previous degree
  • Official transcripts from previous educational institutions
  • Copy of passport (for international students)
  • Personal statement or statement of purpose
  • Letters of recommendation

Subject-Specific Requirements

  • Mathematics proficiency (for Engineering and Science programs)
  • English language proficiency test (TOEFL, IELTS, etc.) for non-native speakers
  • Portfolio submission (for Architecture and Design programs)
  • Relevant entrance exam (if applicable for certain programs)

Credit Transfer Acceptance

Our university accepts credit transfers from recognized institutions, subject to evaluation. The credit transfer process helps students continue their studies smoothly.

  • Credit transfers available for undergraduate and graduate programs.
  • Credits evaluated on a course-by-course basis by the department.
  • Submit official transcripts and course syllabi for evaluation.
  • A maximum of 60 transfer credits may apply to an undergraduate degree.
