Humanities Program

The Humanities program offers a wide range of disciplines focusing on human culture, history, and intellectual thought.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer the following undergraduate programs in Humanities:

  • Bachelor of Arts in English
  • Bachelor of Arts in History
  • Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy
  • Bachelor of Arts in Sociology
  • Bachelor of Arts in Political Science

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs in Humanities provide advanced knowledge in various fields of human culture and intellectual research:

  • Master of Arts in English Literature
  • Master of Arts in History
  • Master of Arts in Sociology
  • Master of Arts in Philosophy
  • PhD in Humanities (Research-based program)

Humanities Courses

The following are some of the core courses offered as part of the Humanities program:

Introduction to Philosophy

An overview of key philosophical ideas, thinkers, and schools of thought.

World History

A study of major historical events that shaped the global world.

Introduction to Sociology

Understanding human society, culture, and the study of social behavior.

Modern Literature

Exploration of significant literary movements and works from the modern era.
