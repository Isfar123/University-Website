Natural Sciences Program

The Natural Sciences program prepares students for careers in scientific research, environmental science, and laboratory technology.

Undergraduate Programs

We offer the following undergraduate programs in the Natural Sciences:

  • Bachelor of Science in Physics
  • Bachelor of Science in Chemistry
  • Bachelor of Science in Biology
  • Bachelor of Environmental Science

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs provide in-depth scientific research skills and knowledge:

  • Master of Science in Physics
  • Master of Science in Environmental Science
  • PhD in Natural Sciences (Research-based program)

Natural Sciences Courses

The following are some of the core courses offered as part of the Natural Sciences program:

Organic Chemistry

Study of carbon compounds and their reactions in organic chemistry.

Environmental Science

Study of the environment, environmental issues, and solutions to environmental problems.

Biology

Study of living organisms, ecology, and cellular biology.

Quantum Mechanics

Introduction to the principles of quantum theory in physics.
